About us

Our Company

Global future are Experts educational consultancy specialising in student recruitment and support with local offices in Egypt to cover all Africa, Middle East region and Gulf Area. Offices are available for students to visit at anytime during the application process or after enrolment for support and guidance. We conduct school tours and presentations, seminars to interfere more with the students and parents.

It is also important to note that we do not charge our students for our services. Everything we do on behalf of our students is completely free of charge. There are no hidden costs.

100% free service

The fact that we do not charge our students leads many to question how we make money as a business. Our model is simple. When one of our students enrols at a UK university, we recieve a commission from the university. This commission is not reflected in the cost that our students pay for their tuition. Students who apply through us pay exactly the same amount as those who apply by themselves. The cost of our service is purely subsidised by the university commission, not our students.

Company Goals

There are six Goals we want to achieve for our customers by 2026:

  1. Inspire and empower people to make aspirational choices about higher education and learning.
  2. Deliver trusted and flexible admissions services for the digital age.
  3. Be the go-to place for higher education data-driven insights.
  4. Connect the world to UK higher education
  5. Embed efficiency and value in everything we do
  6. Be an employer of choice where people flourish, perform, and achieve

Our values
